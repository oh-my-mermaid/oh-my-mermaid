The no-framework approach keeps the viewer dependency-free (beyond the Node.js standard library). The SSE-based live-reload was chosen over WebSockets for simplicity — SSE is unidirectional (server to browser) which is all that's needed for change notifications. The `viewer.html` is a fully self-contained SPA that fetches from the local API; it uses the Mermaid.js CDN to render diagrams client-side without any server-side rendering.
