The decision to keep the Mermaid parser lightweight (regex rather than a full parser) was deliberate — adding a Mermaid parser as a dependency would significantly increase the package size. The current parser covers the subset of Mermaid syntax that omm's own diagram convention uses (`graph LR` with `-->|label|` edges), which is sufficient for diff and validation purposes. The four canonical `classDef` names and their colors (`external`, `concern`, `entry`, `store`) are defined in `validate.ts` as the source of truth and are also documented in the viewer and CLI help text.
